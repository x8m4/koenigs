ABOUT

Who we are?

The SMSbuy is the online bulk SMS service provider working in the state of India for sending bulk messages of any sort to any limit of mobile numbers (from any network provider). Our service enhances the mode of sending transactional and promotional SMS through optimized servers that are actively ready all the time to deliver any message within any timeframe. We offer discounted offers to the new buyers and provide free bulk SMS service for 20 recipients. The service ensures the privacy of the users through our privacy policy, thus making our online tool completely discreet, error-proof, and user-friendly.

Our Journey

The SMSbuy started as a small local service at first but now we have expanded its domain from large cities to small villages with complete access to online messaging service for our users, all according to our terms and conditions.

The SMSbuy offers a gateway to transport online messages to mobile numbers ensuring secrecy, smoothness, efficacy, and express delivery. Moreover, our bulk SMS API can edit, recreate, manage, and re-schedule your messages that are to be delivered effectively and swiftly to the bulk of mobile numbers of any network carrier. Our bulk SMS API can also handle your transport tickets, wedding invitations, and formal greetings. In contrast to other online messaging service providers, SMSbuy is cheaper in cost, smoother in service, and faster in delivery. We assure our users that our servers are secured, optimized, and well-regulated for minimizing the efforts of manual work and saving the time of delivery in contrast to conventional bulk SMS service providers.

Users’ discretion is our basic objective! The users availing the opportunity of bulk SMS service through our website are assured that safety and discretion is our foremost priority. There is a complete end to end encryption for every user; all users have their accounts with unique user ID and password. Thus it makes SMSbuy a completely secure online bulk SMS service.
