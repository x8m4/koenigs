What is Bulk SMS?

Bulk SMS is a thriving service for sending online messages to any mobile number. As from the name, ‘Bulk SMS’ means the dissemination of as many messages as you want to the remote carriers nationwide! It includes Promotional SMS service and Transactional SMS service. It involves the delivery of the online message in the whole country through faster and smoother online servers designed for multiple domains.

Why choose Bulk SMS?

In contrast to the conventional and usual mode of sending messages, bulk SMS provides a new gateway for sending online messages by saving the trouble of sending a large number of messages to many numbers individually. In this way, it saves time and worries of sending the same message to plenty of different numbers in a short time. With one click, you can send a plethora of online messages to any mobile terminal across the whole country.
