What is Transactional SMS?

Transactional SMS are non-marketing messages that tend to provide some significant pieces of information regarding services, orders, and alerts. They may also contain welcome and greeting notes that are sent to the client when he texts the company for the first time.

What is transactional SMS service? :

Transactional SMS service is also a form of bulk SMS service that serves a company or an enterprise for meeting the goals of sending useful information to the clients from time to time. The bulk automated responses (such as greeting messages) for the business also fall under the category of transactional SMS. Now transactional SMS API allows you to monitor the automated service by yourself and it saves the time of manual personalization.

Promotional SMS Vs Transactional SMS:

Usually, the promotional SMS and transactional SMS are considered the same. Yet there is a basic difference between them; the promotional SMS is meant for marketing purposes (e.g. offers and discounts) while the transactional SMS is meant for non-marketing purposes (e.g. billing info etc.).
