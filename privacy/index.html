Log Files

SMS Buy acts upon standard procedures for processing log files. These files log users and visitors and their information when they access this website. Every hosting agency does the same as it is in the analytics of hosting services. The info collected by these files includes IP address, name of the browser, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date and time stamp, and the number of clicks and impressions. These are general pieces of information and are not purposed for the personal identification of the user. The purpose of the collection of such information is demographic data analysis, data traffic management, statistical analysis, and success rate determination of our website.
